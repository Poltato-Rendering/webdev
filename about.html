ABOUT US

We’re a small independent design studio creating minimalist streetwear inspired by anime, manga and Japanese pop culture.

Minimalism as a design philosophy believes in mindfully subtracting the parts that don’t add value while highlighting the beauty in everything that remains. This simplistic approach allows for uncluttered self-expression in every work we produce. Our studio aims to create unconventional yet stylish streetwear designs that blend Japanese pop culture elements with a clean, urban aesthetic.

The name Bakuretsu [爆裂] translates to exploding, which perfectly encapsulates the idea of combining simple but essential components into something that extends beyond itself.

Founded by tsuyoshi [つよし] — a former engineer, minimalist, self-taught graphic designer, and degenerate weeb.
